The challenge

Edteach is a game-based learning platform used as
an educational technology in schools and other educational institutions.
Its learning games are teacher-created multiple-choice quizzes that
can be played by entering a pin and username in a web browser.

Design Development

Unique experience

We worked on design and development. The platform is available on
mobile and web, while the mobile version is in the process of
creation. The web design is made according to the client's requirement
similar to Kahoot.com. The main reason for this is to provide
convenience to former kahoot users. This project has helped us gain
a lot of experience.

How to use?

Edteach is very easy to use, it consists of several steps:
1. Create a test
2. Share a special pin of the test to students
3. Students join the test by entering a special pin
